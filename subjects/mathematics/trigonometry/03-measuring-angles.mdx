
# Measuring Angles

Angles can be measured in:
- **Degrees:** A full circle is 360Â°.
- **Radians:** A full circle is $2\pi$ radians.

## Relationship Between Degrees and Radians

- To convert degrees to radians, use the formula:
  $$\text{radians} = \frac{\text{degrees} \times \pi}{180}$$
- To convert radians to degrees, use the formula:
  $$\text{degrees} = \frac{\text{radians} \times 180}{\pi}$$
